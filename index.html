Creating Icons for future India

ICON Public School is located in the heart of the Vijayawada, i.e, Seetharampuram

It is an affliated school with a stress free environment and balancing the studies and extra curricular activities such as dance, music, yoga and karate.

Our school also runs 2 special programs namely, iSpark and iDynamic. iSpark is to train our kids for competitive exams while iDynamic is to train our students for IIT and JEE exams.

This school works on a cirruculum which includes half-a-day of studies and half-a-day of activities. This makes sure that a student will not get any kind of stress.

We provide books, shoes, uniform and all the basic requirements, including tie, belt, ID card and bags. We have a huge play ground, child's play park, and a volleyball court.

We conduct sports day and we celebrate our annual day with special guests which includes our culture. We also go to educational trips with children to inculcate awareness among them.

We also make our children to participate in inter-school programs.
